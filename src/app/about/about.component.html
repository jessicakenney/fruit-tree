About Us

Fruit Forward is an East Portland based app for quickly reigstering your fruit trees!
Why register your fruit trees? Every year millions of pounds of uneaten fruit are wasted in easily acessable areas. With Fruit Forward you can register your over-abundant fruit trees to help eliminate food waste and hunger! Perhaps you're just a friendly neighbor looking to grab a stray apple or two? That's great too! Fruit Forward is entirely about community collaboration, as a fruit-seeker you can find near-by fruits to obtain, and make a list of your favorite trees.
